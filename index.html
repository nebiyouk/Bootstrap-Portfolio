About Me

My name is Nebiyou Kebede i was born and raised In Addis abeba (Ethiopia). I moved to the United States in 2012, in search of a better opportunity, better life and to pursue my education.

Moving to the United States was not a walk in the park. Once I moved I had to endure the wheatear and the culture shock, The language barrier and finding a stable job. It was a big challeng for a new Comer but through time i came to adjust.

Once I got a stable job and saved enough money, I was ready to fulfill my long time dream which is to finish my education that I started back home, and I got the opportunity to attend University of Pennsylvania Bootcamp to be a Web Developer.
